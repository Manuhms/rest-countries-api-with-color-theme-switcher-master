### Neutral

- Blue 900 (Dark Mode Elements): hsl(209, 23%, 22%)
- Blue 950 (Dark Mode Background): hsl(207, 26%, 17%)
- Grey 950 (Light Mode Text): hsl(200, 15%, 8%)
- Grey 400 (Light Mode Input): hsl(0, 0%, 50%)
